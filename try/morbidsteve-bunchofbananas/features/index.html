Feature Documentation for bunchOfBananas¶

Welcome to bunchOfBananas! Below is a user-friendly guide to the app's key features, organized by purpose. All features are designed to help you manage data, collaborate, and stay organized.

🧱 Core Features¶

1. Home¶

  • What it does: This is your starting point. It provides quick links to your dashboard, recent activity, and app settings.
  • When to use: When you first open the app or need to navigate to other sections.
  • Example: Click the "Dashboard" button on the Home screen to view your project summaries.

2. RootLayout¶

  • What it does: The underlying structure that powers all pages in the app. You won't interact with it directly, but it ensures a consistent look and feel across the app.
  • When to use: Not user-facing, but it supports all other features.

🔄 Dashboard & Data Overview¶

3. DashboardPage¶

  • What it does: Shows a summary of your projects, storage usage, and recent activity.
  • When to use: To get a quick snapshot of your work or check for updates.
  • Example: Use the Dashboard to see how much storage you're using before adding new files.

4. DashboardOverview¶

  • What it does: A visual breakdown of key metrics (e.g., storage, inventory levels) from the DashboardPage.
  • When to use: When you need to analyze trends or spot issues at a glance.
  • Example: The "Storage Usage" chart might show you're nearing your limit, prompting you to upgrade.

🤝 Collaboration Features¶

5. InvitePage¶

  • What it does: Lets you send invitations to others to collaborate on projects.
  • When to use: When you need a teammate or consultant to join your work.
  • Example: Share an invite link with a colleague to let them edit a shared inventory list.

6. AcceptInviteButton¶

  • What it does: Appears when someone receives an invitation. Clicking it joins the inviter's project.
  • When to use: When you receive an invitation link (e.g., via email).
  • Example: A client sends you a link to view their public inventory. Click the button to accept and start collaborating.

📁 Data Management¶

7. InventoryList¶

  • What it does: Lists all items in your inventory with details like quantity, location, and status.
  • When to use: To track what you own or manage stock levels.
  • Example: Use the search bar to find "bananas" and update their count after a shipment arrives.

8. StorageDetailPage¶

  • What it does: Shows detailed info about a specific storage location (e.g., warehouse, cloud folder).
  • When to use: When you need to audit or reorganize stored items.
  • Example: Check the "Cooling Capacity" section to ensure bananas are stored at the right temperature.

9. PricesPage¶

  • What it does: Tracks pricing for items in your inventory or services you offer.
  • When to use: To update costs or generate invoices.
  • Example: Adjust the price of "organic bananas" before creating a customer quote.

🌐 Public Sharing¶

10. PublicViewPage¶

  • What it does: Lets you share read-only views of data (e.g., inventory, prices) with people who don’t need edit access.
  • When to use: When you want to show data to clients, partners, or stakeholders.
  • Example: Share a public link like this:
    https://bunchofbananas.app/public-view/ABC123
    Anyone with the link can view your banana inventory without needing an account.

📊 Advanced Features¶

11. DashboardOverview (Advanced)¶

  • What it does: Offers deeper analytics (e.g., trends, forecasts) for power users.
  • When to use: When you need to export reports or set custom alerts.
  • Example: Export a CSV of storage trends to share with your manager.

Need Help?¶

  • Support: Contact support@bunchofbananas.app
  • Feedback: Use the "Suggest a Feature" button on the Home screen.

Happy managing! 🍌
